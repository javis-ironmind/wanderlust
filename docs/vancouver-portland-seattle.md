# Vancouver/Portland/Seattle Trip Itinerary

## Day 1 - Thu 3/26 - Fly to Seattle
- LAX → SEA (depart 2:00pm)
- Hotel check-in
- Dinner at Bangrak Market (Thai) OR Tendon Kohaku (tempura, Bellevue) at 7pm
- Seattle Pinball Museum (International District)
- Pie Bar (dessert - Capitol Hill)

## Day 2 - Fri 3/27 - Skagit
- Mee Sum Pastry (Pike Place Market, morning)
- Pike Place Market (morning)
- Gum Wall (Post Alley under the market)
- Drive to Skagit (~66 mi, 1h 15m)
- Tulip Festival (Roozengaarde or Tulip Town)

## Day 3 - Sat 3/28 - Kangaroo Farm
- Drive Seattle → Arlington (~40 mi, 1h)
- Outback Kangaroo Farm (10am tour)
- Drive back to Seattle (~40 mi, 1h)
- Artists at Play (Seattle Center playground)
- Shug's Soda Fountain (ice cream, treat)
- Drive Seattle → Mt Rainier area (~90 mi, 1.5h)
- Easy scenic drive through the park OR short easy walk (Narada Falls view, Reflection Lakes)
- Mt. Rainier Scenic Railroad (depart ~2pm, 1h15min ride) - departs from Elbe
- Drive back to Seattle (~90 mi, 1.5h)

## Day 4 - Sun 3/29 - Port Angeles via Capilano
- Capilano Suspension Bridge (North Vancouver)
- Drive to Port Angeles (~90 mi, 2h plus border)

## Day 5 - Mon 3/30 - Port Angeles
- Drive Seattle → Port Angeles (~120 mi, 2.5h)
- Salty Girls Seafood (Sequim, snack/lunch break)
- B&B Family Farm (Sequim lavender farm)
- Arrive at Olympic National Park area

## Day 6 - Tue 3/31 - Olympic NP + Olympia
- Hurricane Ridge OR Hoh Rain Forest OR Olympic Game Farm
- Washington State Capitol (Olympia, tour the grounds)
- Encore Chocolate (Olympia, chocolate shop)
- Chelsea Farms Oyster Bar (Olympia, dinner)
- Overnight in Olympia

## Day 7 - Wed 4/1 - Cannon Beach
- Drive Seattle → Cannon Beach (~150 mi, 2.5h)
- Cannon Beach (Haystack Rock, downtown)
- Drive to Oceanside (~10 mi, 15min)
- Oceanside beach + dinner

## Day 8 - Thu 4/2 - Cannon Beach / Astoria
- Full day at Cannon Beach / Oceanside
- OR day trip to Astoria (~30 mi round-trip)
- Relax, beach, explore

## Day 9 - Fri 4/3 - Portland
- Drive Cannon Beach → Portland (~79 mi, 1.5h)
- Explore Portland
- Witches Castle (Forest Park) — 15min from downtown

## Day 10 - Sat 4/4 - Columbia Gorge + Airplane House
- Drive to Columbia Gorge (~30 mi, 35min)
- Latourell Falls
- Multnomah Falls
- Drive to Hillsboro (~40 mi, 45min)
- Airplane House
- Drive to PDX area (~20 mi, 30min)

## Day 11 - Sun 4/5 - Fly home
- Drive to PDX (~12 mi, 20min)
- Return rental car
- Fly PDX → LAX 6:00am
